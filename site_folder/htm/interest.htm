Interest



















I enjoy ingesting stories through a variety of media.
Reading or listening to books is probably at the top of my list, but I also watch videos, play computer games, and listen to music.
Computer games like StarCraft and StarCraft II have storylines that enhance the game play.

Music, especially Filk music, can also be a great way to enjoy stories.
One way I like to experience Filk music is through concerts at Filk conventions, like Conflikt.
Some artists also post their music on web sites like YouTube.
